About Me

MM

Welcome to my professional portfolio! I'm a Corporate Banker with over 8 years of experience in Supply Chain Finance, currently serving as a Product Manager at YES BANK. My journey has taken me through various roles at prestigious institutions like Tata Capital, where I've specialized in developing innovative financial solutions.

Beyond banking, I'm passionate about languages and cultural exchange. As a certified German Language Trainer with C2 level proficiency from Goethe Institut, I've conducted numerous training sessions, including innovative online courses during the COVID-19 pandemic that connected participants from 5 different countries.

I believe in the power of networking and cultural connections, as demonstrated through my participation in events like "Stuttgart Meets Mumbai," celebrating the ties between Baden-Württemberg and Maharashtra. I'm always excited to connect with professionals and contribute to meaningful projects.
